"Invent. Improve. Overload"

The Technologer is a new custom class focused on technological expantion/evolution. Progress through the game by inventing, improving and overloading.

Contains:
    -New class: The Technologer
        Harness technological weaponry that evolves alongside you.
    -Custom crafting stations
        Upgrade your weaponry through interacting with the different technologer stations.
    -Custom Bosses
        Face new and different bosses that integrate into your progression.
    -New NPCs
        Meet the new NPCs that will help you along your journey.
    -Custom world generation
        Explore custom world generation.
    -Modular expansion
         Designed for smooth integration into modded playthroughs.
